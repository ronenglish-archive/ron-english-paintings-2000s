Literature

Ron English, Son of Pop: Ron English Paints His Progeny (San Francisco, California: 9mm Books and The Shooting Gallery, 2007), p. 29. ISBN 978-0-9766325-1-1.

Notes

This work references Led Zeppelin’s album cover Houses of the Holy.

This composition was later adapted by the artist as two limited-edition prints in the Vinyl Chord: A Revolutionary Record Store series.
