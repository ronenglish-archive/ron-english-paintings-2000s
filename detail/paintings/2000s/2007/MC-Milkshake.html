  • Title: MC Milkshake
  • Date: 2007
  • Medium: Oil on canvas
  • Dimensions: 44 × 84 inches
  • Work ID: RE-000738
  • Signature / marks: Signed “ENGLISH,” lower right.

Exhibitions

Big Picture Pop, Opera Gallery, New York, New York, US, November 29–December 20, 2007.

Literature

Ron English & Morgan Spurlock, Abject Expressionism: The Art of Ron English (San Francisco: Last Gasp, 2007), p. 26. ISBN 978-0867196894.

Ron English, Original Grin: The Art of Ron English (Paris: Cernunnos, 2019), p. 82. ISBN 978-2374950938.

Notes

This work references Ronald McDonald.

This work is documented in the exhibition catalogue for Big Picture Pop.

In Abject Expressionism, this work was erroneously reported as 40 × 90 inches; in Original Grin, it was listed as 40 × 80 inches. This catalogue record retains 44 × 84 inches.

The painting was used as the cover image for Abject Expressionism: The Art of Ron English.

Imagery related to this composition was later used on packaging for MC Supersized Joker action-figure releases.
