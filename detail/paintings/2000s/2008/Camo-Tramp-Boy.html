Notes

This composition was later adapted by the artist as a related hand-embellished screen print in colors on 320gsm Coventry Rag paper, published by Jet Set Graffiti and issued as Camo Tramp Boy (Haliburton Boy).

The composition was also issued as a related serigraph titled Camo Tramp Boy. Art Collectorz records the broader release as 130 total impressions, comprising 100 regular editions, 10 custom hand-painted examples, 10 artist proofs, and 10 printer’s proofs.
