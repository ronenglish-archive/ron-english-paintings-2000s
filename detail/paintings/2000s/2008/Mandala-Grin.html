Notes

The work’s inclusion in Lazarus Rising is documented in the exhibition catalogue.

This work was used as the cover image for Ron English’s Stickable Art Offenses: A Sticker Book, published by Last Gasp in 2011.

A modified banner reproduction of this composition was used in connection with Ron English’s exhibition You Are Not Here at the International Museum of Art and Science, McAllen, Texas, in 2011.
