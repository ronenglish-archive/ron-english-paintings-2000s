Notes

This work appears in the online PDF catalogue for Ron English: Delusionville (Allouche Gallery, 2018).

In the Delusionville exhibition catalogue, this work is erroneously dated 2016.

In Death and the Eternal Forever, the dimensions are erroneously reported as 36 × 36 inches; this catalogue record retains 40 × 40 inches.

A reference sculpture created and photographed by Ron English is retained as supporting documentation for the painting.

The work is also documented in Arrested Motion’s coverage of Delusionville at Allouche Gallery.
