  • Title: Peter and Paul
  • Date: 2008
  • Medium: Oil on canvas
  • Dimensions: 30 × 40 inches
  • Work ID: RE-000851
  • Signature / marks: Signed “ENGLISH,” lower right.

Exhibitions

The Secret History of Kiss, Shooting Gallery, San Francisco, California, US, November 1–December 6, 2008.

POP SURREALISM: What a Wonderfool World at Spoleto, Museo Carandente, Spoleto, Italy, June 26–October 16, 2010.

Literature

Ron English, Death and the Eternal Forever (Korero Press, 2014), p. 44. ISBN 978-0957664920.

Notes

This work references François Alfred Delobbe’s painting The Hearts Awakening.

This painting references members of the band KISS in their signature makeup.

Dorothy Circus Gallery, Artsy, and Singulart list this work with a 2010 date; this catalogue entry retains the 2008 date based on the work’s documented inclusion in The Secret History of Kiss at Shooting Gallery in 2008.
