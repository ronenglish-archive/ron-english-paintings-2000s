Notes

This work appears in Getty Images’ editorial event photographs of Immortal Underground by Ron English at Opera Gallery, New York City.

It also appears in the exhibition catalogue / book The Adam and Ron Show (Elms Lesters Painting Rooms, 2008), which supports its inclusion in the London exhibition.

In Original Grin, Death and the Eternal Forever, and Status Factory, this work was erroneously reported as 47 × 36 inches; this catalogue record retains 48 × 40 inches.
