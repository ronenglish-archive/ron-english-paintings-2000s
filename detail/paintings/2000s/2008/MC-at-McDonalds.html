Notes

Also known as Disco McDonalds and Disco Mac Donalds.

This work references Ronald McDonald.

Third-party auction and marketplace pages document this work under the title Disco Mac Donalds. MutualArt lists the work as oil on canvas, 91 × 61 cm, and signed; Artsy lists oil on canvas, 35 4/5 × 24 × 4/5 inches, and hand-signed lower right. This catalogue entry retains the confirmed 24 × 36 inch measurement and signed lower-right status.
