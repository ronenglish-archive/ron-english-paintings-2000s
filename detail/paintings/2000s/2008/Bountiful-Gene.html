  • Title: Bountiful Gene
  • Date: 2008
  • Medium: Oil on canvas
  • Dimensions: 24 × 36 inches
  • Work ID: RE-000780
  • Signature / marks: Signed “ENGLISH,” lower right.

Exhibitions

The Secret History of Kiss, Shooting Gallery, San Francisco, California, US, November 1–December 6, 2008.

Literature

Ron English, Original Grin: The Art of Ron English (Paris: Cernunnos, 2019), p. 41. ISBN 978-2374950938.

Notes

This work appears in cropped form on Shooting Gallery SF’s gallery page for The Secret History of Kiss.

The composition references William-Adolphe Bouguereau’s painting Charity.

The painting also references Gene Simmons of the band KISS in his signature makeup.

The work is also documented in installation photographs from The Secret History of Kiss published by Arrested Motion.
