Notes

Also known as Super Boy.

This work references DC Comics’ Superman.

This composition was later adapted by the artist as a related digital print in colors on wove paper. The related print appears on Artsy and MutualArt, where it is identified as Super Boy.

The composition was also reproduced as a poster titled Superboy.
