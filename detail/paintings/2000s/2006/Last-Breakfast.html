Notes

This work references Leonardo da Vinci’s The Last Supper.

This work also references a broad group of American breakfast-cereal mascots, including Snap, Crackle and Pop; Captain Horatio Magellan Crunch; the Trix Rabbit; Quisp; Toucan Sam; Tony the Tiger; Lucky the Leprechaun; Count Chocula; Franken Berry; and Boo Berry.

The composition was later reproduced as a poster titled The Last Breakfast.
