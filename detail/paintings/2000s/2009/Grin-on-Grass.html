Exhibitions

Literature

Ron English, Death and the Eternal Forever (London: Korero Press, 2014), p. 57. ISBN 978-0957664920.

Notes

Published as 2009 in Death and the Eternal Forever. Matthew Namour Gallery and Art of AVL both list the work as 2010 and report the dimensions as 36 × 24 inches. This catalogue retains the 2009 date and 24 × 36 inch dimensions based on the published book record and catalogue metadata.

This work references the Peanuts character Charlie Brown, created by Charles M. Schulz.
